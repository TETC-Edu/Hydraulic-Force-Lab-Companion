Why squared? Look at the area.

Force follows the face area of the piston, not its width. Double the diameter and the area grows four times.

Drag the plunger and watch the trade

Drag the teal handle, or press Push. The same fluid that leaves the input fills the output and lifts the load. More force out means less distance out.
